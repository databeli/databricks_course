Genie Name = Bakehouse

Description
This Genie workspace provides AI-powered analytics over bakery franchise sales data, allowing users to analyze revenue, customers, products, and regional performance through natural language queries.

Sample Questions
Identify the top 5 products by revenue in each franchise country.
Show monthly revenue trend by customer continent for the last 12 months.


General Instructions
Business Context
This space contains bakery franchise sales data. Each transaction represents a product sold to a customer at a specific franchise location.

Revenue & Metrics
Revenue = SUM(totalPrice)
Total units sold = SUM(quantity)
Average order value = AVG(totalPrice)
Unique customers = COUNT(DISTINCT customerID)
Unless specified, revenue refers to SUM(totalPrice).

Time Handling
Use dateTime for all time-based analysis.
Monthly → group by month
Yearly → group by year
Daily → group by date
If no time range is specified, use all available data.

Geography
If “location” is mentioned without clarification, default to franchise location.

Defaults
Rankings should be sorted in descending order.
“Top” results default to top 10 unless specified.
Do not expose sensitive information such as full card numbers.


SQL Expressions
Revenue
SUM(sales_transactions.totalPrice)
sales, total sales, total revenue, revenue amount, sales amount, income
Use this expression whenever the user refers to revenue or sales without specifying calculation logic. Always calculate revenue as SUM(sales_transactions.totalPrice). Do not use unitPrice unless explicitly requested.
